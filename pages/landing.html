FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you hit play.

Is Kurovexa really free?
Yes — completely. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no credit card required. We don't even ask for an email if you just want to watch.
Do I need to create an account?
No. You can browse and stream anonymously. An account just unlocks AniList sync, watch history across devices, episode comments and a custom profile.
How does AniList Sync work?
Connect your AniList from the profile page. Every episode you finish on Kurovexa updates your AniList progress, scores and status automatically — no manual updates ever.
What video quality do you offer?
Adaptive quality from 360p up to full HD 1080p. The player picks the right one for your connection automatically, and you can override it anytime from the player settings.
Where does the content come from?
Kurovexa is an indexer — we don't host any video files ourselves. Everything is sourced from public third-party providers and embedded in our player. Check the DMCA page for more details.
Is Kurovexa safe?
Yes. No popups, no redirects, no shady ads. The site itself is clean — we recommend keeping a basic ad-blocker on for any embedded player layer, just to be safe.
